## What This Kit Is

This is a practical cybersecurity starter kit for small UK trades businesses — plumbers, electricians, HVAC engineers, builders, and similar field-service companies.

It is built around a real worked example: **NorthWest Heating & Gas Ltd**, a Manchester plumbing and heating company with 8 employees. They went from zero security policies to Cyber Essentials certified in 90 days, at a total cost of £474. Within four weeks of certification they won a £15,000 council contract that required it.

Everything in this kit is based on what actually works for businesses like yours — not enterprise security guidance repackaged for small businesses.

## Why Cyber Essentials Matters for Trades Businesses

Cyber Essentials is a UK government-backed certification that proves your business takes security seriously. It costs £300 for the basic certification and covers five areas: firewalls, secure configuration, access control, malware protection, and patch management.

**Why it matters for trades businesses specifically:**
- An increasing number of council and commercial contracts require it
- Some cyber insurance policies give discounts for certified businesses
- Customers are starting to ask about data security — especially for businesses that hold home addresses and access details

NorthWest Heating lost a £25,000 council contract because they did not have it. That was their trigger to get it done.
