Why Cyber Essentials matters for trades businesses

Cyber Essentials is a UK government certification that costs £300 and proves your business takes security seriously. An increasing number of council and commercial contracts require it. Some cyber insurance policies give discounts for certified businesses.

NorthWest Heating lost a £25,000 council contract because they did not have it. Within four weeks of getting certified, they won a £15,000 contract that required it. The certification paid for itself many times over.

To apply: Complete this kit first, then apply through an NCSC-approved certification body. IASME is popular for small businesses. The basic self-assessment costs £300.
